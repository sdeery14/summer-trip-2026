# Summer Trip 2026 — Greater Yellowstone Loop

A ~10-day car camping and hiking trip for two, late August 2026. Fly round-trip from Boston to Bozeman, rent a car, loop through Grand Tetons, Yellowstone, and the Beartooth Mountains.

**Route:** BZN → Grand Tetons → Yellowstone NP → Beartooth Highway / Shoshone NF → Red Lodge → BZN

---

## Quick Links — Action Items

These are the things you need to do, roughly in order:

### Right Now (March 2026)
- [ ] [Yellowstone backcountry permits](https://www.nps.gov/yell/planyourvisit/backcountryhiking.htm) — check if lottery is open
- [ ] [Grand Teton backcountry permits](https://www.nps.gov/grte/planyourvisit/back.htm) — check advance reservations
- [ ] [Reserve campsites on recreation.gov](https://www.recreation.gov/) — Jenny Lake (FCFS), Gros Ventre, Signal Mountain, Canyon, Colter Bay
- [ ] [Buy America the Beautiful Pass ($80)](https://store.usgs.gov/pass/index.html) — covers both parks

### April–May
- [ ] [Book flights on Google Flights](https://www.google.com/flights) — BOS → BZN round-trip
- [ ] Book rental car at BZN — midsize SUV (RAV4/Outback class), round-trip

### 1–2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Call Yostmark in Driggs for crash pad rental — (208) 354-2828
- [ ] Book rafting trip if wanted — [Snake River options](features/biking-and-rafting/output.md)
- [ ] Check [Beartooth Highway status](https://www.511mt.net/)
- [ ] Check [Yellowstone road status](https://www.nps.gov/yell/planyourvisit/roads.htm)
- [ ] Check [AirNow for smoke](https://www.airnow.gov/)

### Day of Arrival (Bozeman)
- [ ] Buy bear spray x2 — REI Bozeman or Schnees
- [ ] Buy fuel canisters for camp stove
- [ ] Walmart grocery run — N 7th Ave, Bozeman

## The Itinerary at a Glance

| Day | Where | Sleep | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|-------|
| **0** | Fly in → BZN → drive south | Jackson area or Gros Ventre | Bear spray, groceries, drive to Tetons via Gallatin Canyon (~3.5 hrs) |
| **1** | Grand Tetons | Jenny Lake / Gros Ventre | Big day hike — Paintbrush-Cascade loop or Death Canyon Shelf |
| **2** | Grand Tetons | Same camp | Bouldering at Teton Boulder Park (Driggs) or second big hike |
| **3** | Grand Tetons | Same camp or Jackson | Flex — hike, backpack, raft, or chill. Celebration dinner in Jackson |
| **4** | Tetons → Yellowstone | Canyon / Bridge Bay | Drive north through park (~2-3 hrs), stop at West Thumb, Lewis Falls. Afternoon hike or geothermal |
| **5** | Yellowstone Core | Canyon / Bridge Bay | Grand Canyon of Yellowstone, Grand Prismatic, Norris Geyser Basin |
| **6** | Yellowstone North | Slough Creek / Tower Fall | Dawn Lamar Valley wildlife, day hike — Specimen Ridge or Slough Creek |
| **7** | Yellowstone → Beartooth | Red Lodge or Beartooth Lake CG | Drive NE entrance → Beartooth Highway (the drive IS the activity). Red Lodge evening |
| **8** | Beartooth / Shoshone NF | Beartooth Lake CG or Red Lodge | Alpine hiking on the plateau — Beauty Lake, Line Creek Plateau |
| **9** | Beartooth / Shoshone NF | Same camp | Optional backpacking in Beartooth Wilderness, or second day hike |
| **10** | Red Lodge → BZN | Fly home | 1 hr drive to airport |
